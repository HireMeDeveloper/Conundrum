How To Play
A daily game with 3 puzzles
In game 1 you are given 7 letters. Your task is to arrange all of those letters to form a word. This will require lateral thinking. You have 30 seconds to guess the correct answer.
Game 2 has 8 letters and you have 40 seconds. Game 3 you have 9 letters and 50 seconds.
Hints
A hint button is included to help you guess the word. However your grading will recieve a penalty if you use the Hint function!
